Grand Prize Awarded at 2018 Graduation Exhibition Contest

Sangjun Yoon, Seunghan Lee and Hoyoung Kim in the Lab were awarded Grand Prize at 2018 Graduation Exhibition Contest on November 20, 2018. Graduation Exhibition Contest is annually organized by Student Association for Department of Software and Communications Engineering and sponsored by Department of Software and Communications Engineering. In the contest, students compete vigorously with their systems which have been developed based on their four-year learnings and skills. Based on machine learning algorithms which are recent AI technologies, the team developed Firewall System for Automatic Policy Rule Generation based on Artificial Intelligence. This system solved fatal problems of existing firewall systems. They have been advised under both Prof. Seong Oun Hwang and Kyung Hyun Han, the Lab leader.

Research Fellows and Post-Docs

We are seeking research fellows and post-doctoral fellows with high impact journal publications. To get full fund, they shall apply for the Korean government programs, the BP (Brain Pool) and KRF (Korea Research Fellow) programs, respectively, under our support. Research fellows are required to have been awarded a doctoral degree for more than five years and they should work in Korea for (min) 6 months - (max) 3 years if they are selected. Post-doctoral fellows are required to have been awarded a doctoral degree within the past five years as of application date and should work in Korea for (min) 3 years - (max) 5 years if they are selected. Accepted BP applicants will receive salary of (min) 4,250 USD up to (max) 21,250 USD including taxes. The exact amount of salary payment will be determined as equivalent to the applicant's salary of past year prior to selection for the BP program. Besides the salary, applicants will be supported air fee, moving expenses, insurances, etc. Accepted KRF applicants will receive salary of 2,970 USD including taxes. Applicants are required to send emails with resume (including academic transcripts and publication records) to seongoun dot hwang at gmail dot com.
